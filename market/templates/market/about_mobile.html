The Origin of LinkHash

LinkHash originally launched as a crypto reward-based task platform where users earned rewards by completing simple and verifiable tasks. Inspired by how the Bitcoin network rewards miners for discovering valid hashes through computation, LinkHash was designed to mirror that concept in the online world.

This led to the name "LinkHash" — combining "Link" (connections, actions, engagement) and "Hash" (verification, proof, crypto-native rewards).
Example tasks
  • Following specific social media accounts
  • Posting themed content on their own feeds
  • Participating in surveys
  • Downloading apps and leaving reviews
Verified users received rewards in USDT and SOL. From day one, LinkHash aimed to become a Web3-native reward-driven marketing platform.
